Poetic Computation

Poetic Computation: Reader is an online-book by Taeyoon Choi about code as a form of poetry and aesthetic and ethical questions associated with code. The book is based on Choiâ€™s lectures at the School for Poetic Computation, and independent school he co-founded in New York City. The first two chapters are published on September, 2017 with following chapters to be published over the year. Poetic Computation: Reader presents new possibilities of web browser to enhance accessibility. The book is designed by HAWRAF to enhance accessibility and legibility to readers. Readers can change the design elements and formats to meet their ideal reading experience. Poetic Computation: Reader is edited by Hannah Son, and includes interviews with a select group of scholars and practitioners.
